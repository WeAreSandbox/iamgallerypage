The Event

Sandbox hosts Jayu's celebration of the "I Am" project which featured six Toronto-based photographers and six youth experiencing homelessness out of the Horizon's For Youth shelter.

The project empowered the youth to share their personal stories throught he photography skills they acquired from the project. Each week a different photographer held a workshop focusing on a different element of mobile street photography. At the end of each workshop the youth had an opportunity to go on a photowalk where they were able to practice their photography skills and build their story. Over a period of several weeks the youth's photos were displayed in multiple art galleries across the city where 100% of the proceeds from each photo sale went directly back to the youth who captured the photo.

The project has helped these youth feel safe in their own minds and we'd like to celebrate that. Next year Jayu aims to connect with 50 individuals experiencing homelessness, in an effort to celebrate what was and what will be, come out and see a visual summary of the project.
